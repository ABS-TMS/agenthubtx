Security
Client Data Handling Policy & Email Security Checklist
Protecting client data on your accounts and devices
+
Why it matters
Real estate transactions involve sensitive client financial and personal information, and agents are frequent targets for wire fraud and phishing schemes tied to closings.
What to do
  1. Use two-factor authentication on your email, CRM, and transaction management accounts
  2. Never act on wiring instructions received only by email — verify by phone using a number you already have on file
  3. See the full Email Security Checklist for the exact verification script to use before a closing
Still stuck? This page covers the how — for the why behind a policy or a TREC requirement, check the FAQ Resource. For anything this page doesn't answer, reach out to your designated supervisor first, before escalating further.
