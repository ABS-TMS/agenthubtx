How to Use TDI's Report Tool

TDI's system generates downloadable reports rather than instant search results.

  1. Go to TDI's report tool (link above)
  2. In the left menu, click Agent Licensing - Escrow Officers and Title Agencies
  3. Choose a report — for most purposes, "Licensed Title Agents/Direct Operations by County" or the Underwriter report (shows which underwriters an agent is approved to write for)
  4. Click Run Report, then search or filter for the county you need

What's Regulated, and by Whom

TDI licenses and regulates title insurance agents, direct operations, and escrow officers — including their standing, bonding, and continuing education requirements.

TREC has no authority here. The contract requires a title company's name and address for practical closing purposes, but TREC doesn't license or regulate title companies — that's entirely TDI's jurisdiction.
